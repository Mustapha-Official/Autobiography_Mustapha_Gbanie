## 👨‍💻 About This Project
This is my official biography and autobiography website — built to share my journey, skills, and story with the world.  
I created **every part of this website myself**, coding **only on a mobile phone** (no laptop, no computer), proving that with faith and hard work, you can build anything from anywhere.

This website tells:
🌺Who I am & where I’m from (Tungie, Gorama Mende, Kenema, Sierra Leone)
🌺How I learned web development with limited resources
🌺My inspiring journey & challenges I overcame
🌺My services — building websites for businesses, shops, brands & organizations
🌺My vision & goals for the future
🌺Inspiration notes & answers to questions people ask about my life
